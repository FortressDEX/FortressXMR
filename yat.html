Yat wallet integration is coming to MyMonero in 2022

MyMonero users can now buy a Yat via MyMonero

Development is underway to add additional Yat functionality, to be released early next year

Soon, MyMonero users will be able to manage and connect any of your Yats to your selected MyMonero wallet, so that Monero payments can be made using your Yat
