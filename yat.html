Yat wallet integration is coming to FortressXMR in 2022

FortressXMR users can now buy a Yat via FortressXMR

Development is underway to add additional Yat functionality, to be released early next year

Soon, FortressXMR users will be able to manage and connect any of your Yats to your selected FortressXMR wallet, so that Monero payments can be made using your Yat
